ABOUT US

Bite & Spice is a recipe hub designed for beginners, students, and anyone looking for simple, affordable cooking solutions. We believe that cooking at home should be easy, fun, and budget-friendly. Our mission is to inspire new cooks to embrace the kitchen and try new things, without spending a lot of money. Founded by Aiman Karami, Bite & Spice offers a collection of easy-to-follow recipes and kitchen tips that anyone can master. From quick meals for busy days to affordable ingredients, we’ve got everything you need to get cooking. Join us in the kitchen, and let’s make every meal a tasty adventure!
