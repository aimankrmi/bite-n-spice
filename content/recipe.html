Burger with melted cheese

Ingredients

11/2 cups all-purpose flour
31/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
1 large egg
3 tablespoons melted butter
11/4 cups milk

Instructions

Step 1

Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
