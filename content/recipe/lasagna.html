Sausage Gravy Lasagna

Ingredients

Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
8 ounces lasagna noodles (about 12)
3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for tossing and greasing the baking dish
2 pounds bulk breakfast sausage, thawed
1 bunch scallions, whites cut into 1/2-inch pieces and greens thinly sliced
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
4 cups whole milk
1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
2 cups shredded low-fat mozzarella cheese
8 ounces white Cheddar, shredded
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
Two 10-ounce packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and excess liquid squeezed out
1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Instructions

Step 1

Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the noodles as per the package instructions. Drain, toss with a little oil, and set aside to cool.

Step 2

Heat 3 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Cook sausage and scallion whites, breaking the sausage into small chunks, until browned (about 8 minutes). Sprinkle flour over the sausage and mix. Add 2 cups of milk, stir until thickened, then add the other 2 cups of milk. Simmer until the sauce coats the back of a spoon (about 2 minutes). Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Remove from heat and let cool.

Step 3

Mix mozzarella, Cheddar, and Parmesan cheeses in a bowl.

Step 4

Oil a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom. Layer as follows:

  • 4 noodles (slightly overlapping)
  • 1/3 of the sauce
  • 1/2 of the spinach
  • 1/3 of the cheese mix

Repeat this layering twice, finishing with the last layer of noodles, sauce, and cheese on top.

Step 5

Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.

Step 6

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Remove plastic wrap, cover with foil, and bake for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the top is golden. Garnish with scallion greens, parsley, and black pepper. Let rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

TIPS

When to serve
Let the lasagna rest for at least 15–20 minutes after baking. This helps it set, making it easier to slice and serve without falling apart. Plus, the flavors meld together beautifully during this time!
